What is Mr. Fusion?

Created by the brilliant minds in Brown's Laboratory, the Mr. Fusion Home Energy Reactor is the solution for creating clean energy. By using isotopes of the element hydrogen and combining them with small nuclei in the process, the Mr. Fusion Home Energy Reactor is able to create safe energy that can easily outpace the raw output of plutonium.

Our scientists spent years testing and perfecting the viability of the reactor to ensure it met the needs of many. As waste continues to pile up in our world, it is important to be mindful and proactive of how it is disposed. By dissolving deuterium into a solid, palladium metal at or near room temperature, the Mr. Fusion Home Energy Reactor is able to generate reusable energy that is both clean and one-hundred times more efficient than what nuclear reactors create nowadays. The energy is not just used for one type of machine either; cars, microwaves, home outlets, and more can be powered through the incredible power of our cold fusion reactor. This allows for a healthier atmosphere and better quality of life to whatever product it is applied to. Our reactors are capable of this through a special form of regenerative atoms that can rejuvinate machinery back into its prime.
