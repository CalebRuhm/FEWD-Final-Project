What is Mr. Fusion?

Created by the brilliant minds in Brown's Laboratory, the Mr. Fusion Home Energy Reactor is the solution for creating clean energy. By using isotopes of the element hydrogen and combining them with small nuclei in the process, the Mr. Fusion Home Energy Reactor is able to create safe energy that can easily outpace the raw output of plutonium.
